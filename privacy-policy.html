Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

QuickSell ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Photos you upload: Images you submit for price estimation. These are sent to our AI processing service (Google Gemini) and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Device identifier: A randomly generated anonymous ID stored on your device to manage your scan balance. This is not linked to your identity.
  • Usage data: Anonymous scan counts and app usage patterns to improve the service.
  • Error logs: Crash reports and error data collected via Sentry to help us fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered price estimates for items you photograph
  • To manage your free and paid scan balance
  • To improve app performance and fix errors
  • To prevent abuse of our service

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Google Gemini API: Images are sent for AI analysis. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Cloudflare: Our backend infrastructure. Privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy
  • Sentry: Error tracking. Privacy policy: sentry.io/privacy

4. Data Storage

Your scan history and collection data are stored locally on your device. Your scan balance is stored on our secure Cloudflare servers tied to your anonymous device ID. Photos are processed in real-time and not permanently stored.

5. Children's Privacy

QuickSell is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Clear your local data at any time via device settings
  • Request deletion of your server-side balance data by contacting us

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, signed API requests, and rate limiting to protect your data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
